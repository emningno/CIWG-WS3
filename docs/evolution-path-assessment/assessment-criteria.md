# Assessment Criteria

{: .draft }
> Draft – criteria are proposed and subject to WS3 agreement.

Scenarios are evaluated against the following criteria. Each criterion is 
scored on a three-point scale (Low / Medium / High) in the 
[Scenario Assessment](scenario-assessment).

| # | Criterion | Description | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | Regulatory compliance | Degree to which the scenario satisfies eIDAS 2.0, ViDA, EUBW obligations | High |
| C2 | Backward compatibility | Impact on existing SPs and their operational infrastructure | High |
| C3 | Ecosystem readiness | Realistic availability of required standards, tooling, and expertise | Medium |
| C4 | Migration complexity | Effort and risk associated with transition | Medium |
| C5 | Future-proofing | Degree to which the scenario avoids near-term re-architecture | Medium |
| C6 | Governance fit | Alignment with Peppol Authority model and OpenPeppol governance capacity | Medium |
