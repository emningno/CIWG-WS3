## Problem statement

_Why does the current flat participant model create trust policy challenges?
What regulatory or operational requirements demand differentiation?_

## Objectives of segmentation

1. Enable proportionate trust requirements without lowest-common-denominator policy
2. Support regulatory differentiation (e.g. ViDA mandatory e-invoicing vs voluntary use)
3. Provide a framework for introducing higher-assurance tiers without disrupting base operations
4. Align with EUBW participant categories where applicable
