# ERDS Requirements

{: .draft }
> Draft – requirements to be extracted from final eIDAS 2.0 text (Reg. 2024/1183).

This page lists the normative ERDS requirements that form the basis of the gap analysis.
Requirements are numbered for cross-referencing in the [Gap Register](gap-register).

Requirements are drawn from:
- Article 44 (Electronic registered delivery services)
- Article 45 (Qualified electronic registered delivery services)
- Annex IV (Requirements for qualified electronic registered delivery services)

---

## Article 44 – Non-qualified ERDS

| Req # | Source | Requirement summary |
|---|---|---|
| ERDS-01 | Art. 44(1)(a) | Identification of the sender with high degree of confidence |
| ERDS-02 | Art. 44(1)(b) | Identification of the addressee before delivery |
| ERDS-03 | Art. 44(1)(c) | Data integrity of content |
| ERDS-04 | Art. 44(1)(d) | Date and time of sending and receiving |
| ERDS-05 | Art. 44(1)(e) | Protection against risk of loss, theft, damage, or unauthorised alteration |

## Article 45 / Annex IV – Qualified ERDS

| Req # | Source | Requirement summary |
|---|---|---|
| QERDS-01 | Annex IV (a) | Provided by qualified trust service providers |
| QERDS-02 | Annex IV (b) | Sender identity verified at high level of assurance |
| QERDS-03 | Annex IV (c) | Addressee identity verified before delivery |
| QERDS-04 | Annex IV (d) | Data sealed with qualified electronic seal |
| QERDS-05 | Annex IV (e) | Any change in data made detectable |
| QERDS-06 | Annex IV (f) | Date and time indicated by qualified timestamp |
| QERDS-07 | Annex IV (g) | Trusted third party records transmission |
